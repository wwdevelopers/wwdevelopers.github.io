Cases

Below are few life examples of our work to let you know how we are supporting all the non-technologists friends.

Graphic design A sort of beauty

Some person asked us for help with logo and color set for his website. We gave a guide about how to suit brand to the company sector.

Web development Boost me all

The question was related to poor performance of company website. We took the challenge and gave to the requestor a package of support. We created a site map for better understanding of the structure, made several performance tests, added site to monitor tools and finally created a step by step guide of what have to be repaired, upgraded and implemented to have the performance at best. This was combination of web development and administration services cause requestor ended up with new server and database.

Infrastructure Slow motion

We were asked for support in choosing new infrastructure for website. Current server was overloaded and cannot be scaled up to fit the requirements of growing traffic. Our help came down to deep report of services currently used and few solutions for the future prospects.

Graphic design What's going on

The starting point here was poor conversion rate but going forward the challenge was terrible UX of website. After a little workshops we proposed a sort of changes within site structure. The list consisted of about 60 points of tasks from small like lack of form validation to bigger things like huge number of 404 errors.

Infrastructure Improve my pet house

It was just a question about best possible server-database combination for low traffic website. Got to mention that the person who asked did not have much funds to run the idea. We took our expertise and gave cheap but great solution for running the project. We were asked also for help with relocation of resources which we did at no costs but with huge satisfaction. The idea was a charity fund for homeless animals.

Web development I can't find myself

The issue was poor search results of some website. After quick research we noticed that said website did not have SEO basics included in HEAD section, also the structure could be improved in a few ways. We did a report and make a guide for future implementation. Just for fun of being helpful we added required META stuff and linked site to Google Analytics.
