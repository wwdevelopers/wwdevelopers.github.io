Common Cases

Below are few life examples of our work to let you know how we are supporting all the non-technologists friends.

Graphic design A sort of beauty

We got questions about logos and websites color sets. We advised how to give a brand an identity suitable for the company sector. We gave guidance how right design can enhance its positive image.That included website's target audience analysis, visual campaign main goals, figuring out the company's mission and simply choosing the best shapes and colors that fitted.

Web development Boost me all

We receive questions related to poor performance of companies websites. We took up the challenge and prepared a package of support. We created a site map for better understanding of the structure, made several performance and structure tests on GTMetrix, PageSpeed or Lighthouse, we scanned site with securityheaders.io, made load/stress test, added site to monitoring tool - Zabbix via Grafana and finally created a step by step guide of what have to be repaired, upgraded and implemented to have the performance at its best. This comes as a combination of web development and administration services and some requestors ended up with new server or database.

Infrastructure Slow motion

We were asked for support in choosing new infrastructure for a website. Current server was overloaded and could not be scaled up to fit the requirements of growing traffic. Our help came down to deep report of services currently used and a few solutions for the future prospects.

Graphic design What's going on

The starting point here was poor conversion rate but going forward the challenge was terrible UX of website. After a little workshops we proposed a sort of changes within site structure. The list consisted of about 60 points of tasks like lack of form validation, responsiveness issues, heavy images or huge number of 404 errors.

Infrastructure Improve my pet house

Help was needed to find best possible server-database combination for a low traffic website with poor budget. We came up with 5$/month VPS which could handle up to 500 visits per minute. We assisted with relocation of resources at no cost but with huge satisfaction.

Web development I can't find myself

The issue were poor search results of a website. After a quick research we noticed that the website did not have SEO basics included in the HEAD section, also the structure could be improved in a few ways. We did a report and made a guide for future implementation. Just for joy of being helpful we added required META stuff and linked site to Google Analytics.
